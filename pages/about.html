Bonjour, Mes Amies!

Despite being born in the States, our family's history traces back to the farm country of France where our ancestors grew their own wheat to bake bread and sell it at the daily Farmer's Market in the village.

Today we proudly carry our family's tradition of baking fresh baguettes daily and over the years we have added a variety of authentically French items to our baked goods selection.

So, come on by and relax under an umbrella on our patio with the freshest baked goods you can imagine amidst the stunning Utah mountain backdrop and then you'll understand why our slogan is...

"La Petite Pâtisserie de Paris is where
you'll feel like you're in the heart of France,
even though you're in the heart of Utah".

In addition to our daily selections, ask us about our custom desserts and catering services for your special occasions.

Newly added this year, you can also take advantage of the following services:
  • Macarons To-Go Service -- Text Us Your Order
  • Contactless Curbside Pick-Up
  • Ship the Taste of France Directly to Your Door
  • Virtual Mommy & Me Baking Classes -- Learn Some of Our Baking Secrets and Have Fun Too!
